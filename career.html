Logic ERP Training

Welcome to Mihu Infotech, your trusted destination for comprehensive SAP ERP software training. We specialize in providing top-notch training programs designed to equip individuals and organizations with the skills and knowledge required to leverage SAP's powerful enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions. With our experienced instructors and hands-on approach, we ensure that you gain the expertise needed to excel in the SAP ecosystem.

Why Choose Mihu Infotech for ERP Training?


Experienced and Certified Instructors:

Learn from highly experienced and certified ERP professionals who possess extensive industry knowledge. Our instructors bring real-world insights into the classroom, ensuring a practical and industry-relevant learning experience.

Comprehensive Curriculum:

Our training programs cover a wide range of ERP modules, including finance and controlling (FICO), supply chain management (SCM), human capital management (HCM), sales and distribution (SD), and more. You will gain a holistic understanding of ERP, empowering you to meet diverse business needs.

Hands-On Learning:

Gain practical experience through hands-on exercises, case studies, and real-world simulations. Our training includes access to ERP systems and sandbox environments, allowing you to apply your knowledge in a practical setting.

Customized Training Solutions:

We understand that every organization and individual has unique requirements. That's why we offer customized training solutions tailored to your specific needs. We can deliver training onsite, online, or through blended learning approaches to ensure maximum flexibility and effectiveness.

Certification:

We provide a certificate on completion of training at Logic ERP which help you to get more opportunities in the career. And also placement is provided to the trainee in respective areas.

Training Programs Offered:

  • ERP Financial Accounting (FI)
  • ERP Controlling (CO)
  • ERP Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • ERP Materials Management (MM)
  • ERP Production Planning (PP)
  • ERP Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • ERP Business Intelligence (BI)
  • ERP Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
